Meanwhile, our creative vision for some of our most popular lines, like our Mala Collection, offers a fresh look in a saturated market of wood bracelets. JT continues to combine the highest quality of materials to create some of the most fashionable and iconic hip hop and streetwear items that redefine the standards of men's bracelets.

HIP HOP PENDANTS

THE JT QUALITY: HIP HOP PENDANTS

JT delivers the most fashionable hip hop pendants because of our promise of combining inventive designs with the most cutting-edge technology. Each pendant, from its conception right to its final design, takes months of planning, development and revisions prior to reaching your door steps. All of our men's pendants come plated in 14K or 18K gold or rhodium to provide the best shine and protection possible. Meanwhile, our pendants include hand set, CZ stones that come placed along a micro pave setting to create better design layouts. And with the JT logo laser engraved on each pendant, everyone will know who you're repping!

MEN'S EARRINGS

MEN'S DIAMOND EARRINGS

Our diamond earrings use 14K solid gold or .925 sterling silver to provide the best shine and quality possible. Our hip hop men's earrings, like our Diamond Collection, uses real diamonds cut into a round or princess style. Our male earrings all use hand-set stones placed along a micro pave or prong setting, while our casted silver studs use CZ stones with gold, black or rose-gold plated coatings. Although fashion in general can go through trends in a matter of months, men's earrings tend to remain in style for years. Today's men's diamond stud earrings for example, look practically identical to those from the 1980s when hip hop music became popular. For jewelry you plan to use for years to come, there's no better choice than diamond stud earrings for men.

HIP HOP WATCHES

THE JT QUALITY: HIP HOP WATCHES

All of our hip hop watches take inspiration from their high-end counterparts, outfitted with CZ stones, built with large, eye-catching watch faces and lined with 14K gold. And just like our JT Pendants, each watch spends months in development prior to being approved for production, and by using only the highest quality of materials, we're able to provide the best possible product. Whether it's chronograph or men's gold watches, JT provides an assortment of options.

HIP HOP RINGS

THE JT QUALITY: HIP HOP RINGS

From rose-gold rings to bling rings, JT's collection of style, colors and designs takes its inspiration from high-end designers and a stable of creative minds that continue to produce the most contemporary, iconic rings for the hip hop and streetwear communities. From our collection of Premium Rings to our plush Vintage Collection, JT excels at meeting and exceeding the needs and expectations of the hip hop and streetwear communities. Whether it's from our .925 sterling silver hip hop rings or from our skull-designed items, JT offers one-of-a-kind products that will draw attention, no matter if you're walking the streets of your hometown or touring the clubs.

HIP HOP CHAINS

THE JT QUALITY: HIP HOP CHAINS

Carrying the widest assortment of hip hop chains on the Web, JT continues to provide a collection of modern and retro-style chains that pay tribute to our hip-hop and streetwear roots, from our classic Francos and Yellow-Gold chains to our more modern Rosaries and Supernatural chains. Our stable of men's hip hop chains typically come in three sizes--24", 30" and 36"--with five main color variants: Gold, Yellow-Gold, Rose-Gold, Blackout and Silver (Silver plated or Sterling Silver). And each hip hop chain color and size appeals to whatever outfit or night out you have planned, from our more conservative yellow-gold styles to our more eye-catching hip hop chains like our iced-out Pharaoh line. Whether its attention you seek or a style to complement your look, JT provides an array of men's hip hop chains in various widths (from as small as 1mm to as large as 20mm), sizes and colors for you to pick from.

ABOUT JT

THE JT QUALITY

JT creates its hip hop chains, pendants, bracelets and earrings using the most cutting-edge technology in the industry to create hip hop jewelry that captures the spirit of the music we listen to and love. Our products come dipped 5 times in 14K or 18K gold or rhodium plating to prevent tarnishing, while our .925 sterling silver necklaces and pendants provide style--and quality—you can trust. With pendants and chains using hand-set CZ stones that come placed along a micro pave setting, and earrings using real diamonds, our products include the highest quality of materials. Our hip hop pendants, meanwhile, go through months of revisions and planning before ever reaching your doorstep. And all of our pendants forgo the use of traditional mold-based castings to redefine the standards of jewelry making, a standard that grants higher-quality jewelry with more intricate designs. Some jewelry manufacturers look to follow the standards, but at JT, we aim to define the standards of jewelry making. OUR PROMISE TO OUR CUSTOMERS At JT, our aim isn’t to sell you jewelry; our goal is to sell the most fashionable, iconic men's hip hop jewelry products on the market. You represent our jewelry, and we represent you. Because of this notion, we aim to foster relationships with our clients, via our social media accounts, through our customer support experience and through our products to give our loyal fans an experience that will be cherished and remembered.
